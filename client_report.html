How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

I decided what content and images to use for my site plan by thinking about what kind of site I want to use and that was social media for crafting. I chose to use images of my crafts because they match the concept of the site.

How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

I tried to communicate with my developer through email, canvas, and teams. The issue with communication was that my leader developer never answered me and so I wasn't able to communicate with him. My junior developers have answered me but only one of them has responded to me multiple times.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

My leader developer and one of my junior developers did not meet my expectations for my site because they didn't do the work. Another junior developer has met my expectations for my site by doing her part in making my site.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

Next time I hire a team to do a project for me, I would find a team that replies to me and does the work. Also, I will make a wireframe that is more detailed so that my hired team will understand better about how I want my site to look.
